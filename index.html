Simple Steps

How DiKeVoi! Works

Get from A to B with friends and split the cost in three simple steps.

01

Post or Find a Ride

Enter your pickup and drop-off location on campus. Choose to offer a ride or search for an existing one going your way.

02

Match & Confirm

DiKeVoi! matches you with students travelling the same route. Accept the match, confirm your seat, and you're good to go.

03

Ride & Split

Enjoy the ride! When you arrive, the app automatically calculates each person's fair share of the total fare.
